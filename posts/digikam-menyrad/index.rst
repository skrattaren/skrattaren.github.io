How to enable menubar in `digiKam <http://www.digikam.org/>`__ once
you've disabled it accidentally?

 ***sterkrig@trollsdatorn ~ $** nano ~/.kde/share/config/digikamrc*

::

    [MainWindow]

    MenuBar=Enabled
